Who Are We?

Raceeducare, established in 2011 and approved by Nepal’s Ministry of Education, is your trusted study abroad consultancy based in Manbhawan, Kumaripati Lalitpur. We offer expert guidance for students aiming to study in top global institutions across countries like Australia, Finland, the UK, and the USA. Our services include course and university selection, application support, scholarship assistance, test preparation for IELTS, TOEFL, and Duolingo, visa and documentation help, and pre-departure briefing. With a focus on excellence, we provide personalized support to help you achieve your academic and career goals through world-class education opportunities.
